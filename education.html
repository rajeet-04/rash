ICSE

I, Rajeet Ash, from Dreamland School, Makhla, successfully cleared the ICSE Class 10 exam in 2021 with an overall score of 89.6%. I excelled in English and Hindi, securing full marks (99 out of 100) in both languages. Additionally, I achieved a commendable score of 89 in Mathematics. I also performed well in History, Civics, Geography, Science, and Computer Applications. My Internal Assessment was graded 'Grade A.'
